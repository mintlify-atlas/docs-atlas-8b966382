Hollow Cassettes are special cassettes that can be linked to [Viewfinders](/blocks/viewfinder) to display live camera feeds on [Televisions](/blocks/television).

## Overview

Unlike regular [Cassette Tapes](/items/cassette-tapes) which play pre-recorded videos, Hollow Cassettes create a live connection to a Viewfinder, allowing you to broadcast what the viewfinder is seeing in real-time on any TV.

<Card title="Key Features" icon="video">
  - Link to viewfinders to create live camera feeds
  - Display the viewfinder's view on any television
  - Always provides maximum redstone signal (15)
  - Shows the linked location in the tooltip
  - Glows with enchantment effect when linked
</Card>

## Crafting

<CodeGroup>
```yaml Recipe
Type: Shapeless Crafting
Ingredients:
  - 1x Cassette (any)
  - 1x Echo Shard
Result:
  - 1x Hollow Cassette
```
</CodeGroup>

To create a Hollow Cassette, combine any regular cassette with an Echo Shard in a crafting table. The cassette will be consumed and converted into a Hollow Cassette.

<Note>
You can use any regular cassette tape for this recipe - the video content doesn't matter as it will be replaced with the live feed.
</Note>

## Linking to a Viewfinder

### Steps to Link

1. **Place a [Viewfinder](/blocks/viewfinder)** in the location you want to broadcast from
2. **Hold the Hollow Cassette** in your hand
3. **Right-click on the Viewfinder** with the cassette
4. The cassette will glow and display the linked coordinates in its tooltip

<Tip>
Once linked, the Hollow Cassette will display a glowing enchantment effect and show the viewfinder's coordinates when you hover over it.
</Tip>

### Viewing the Feed

1. Take your linked Hollow Cassette to any [Television](/blocks/television)
2. Power the TV with redstone
3. Right-click the TV with the Hollow Cassette to insert it
4. The TV will now display the live feed from the linked Viewfinder

<Warning>
The Viewfinder must be in a loaded chunk for the feed to display. If the viewfinder's chunk is unloaded, the feed will not be available.
</Warning>

## Tooltip Information

Hollow Cassettes display helpful information in their tooltip:

- **When linked**: Shows the exact coordinates `[X, Y, Z]` of the linked viewfinder
- **When linked (far away)**: Shows the dimension name if in a different dimension
- **When invalid**: Shows "Linked to invalid location" if the viewfinder was removed or is in an unloaded chunk

## Redstone Output

Hollow Cassettes always provide the maximum comparator signal strength of **15** when inserted into a TV, regardless of whether they're linked or not. This is the highest signal strength of any cassette type, making them useful for redstone contraptions.

<CodeGroup>
```yaml Comparator Values
Regular Cassettes: 1-11 (varies by cassette)
Hollow Cassettes: 15 (always)
```
</CodeGroup>

## Technical Details

### Broadcast System

Hollow Cassettes use the mod's broadcast system to create a connection between the viewfinder and the TV:

1. Each Viewfinder has a unique UUID identifier
2. When you link a Hollow Cassette, it stores the viewfinder's UUID
3. When inserted into a TV, it looks up the viewfinder by UUID
4. The TV renders the viewfinder's camera perspective in real-time

### Cross-Dimensional Links

Hollow Cassettes can be linked to viewfinders in different dimensions:

- The link stores the global position (dimension + coordinates)
- The tooltip will show the dimension name when viewing from another dimension
- The feed will only work if the source dimension is loaded

<Note>
In multiplayer, the viewfinder's chunk needs to be in a loaded area (spawn chunks, force-loaded chunks, or near active players) for the feed to work.
</Note>

## Relinking Cassettes

You can relink a Hollow Cassette to a different viewfinder at any time:

1. Right-click a different Viewfinder with the linked Hollow Cassette
2. The cassette will update to link to the new viewfinder
3. The old link is replaced - you cannot link one cassette to multiple viewfinders

<Tip>
If you need multiple feeds, craft multiple Hollow Cassettes and link each one to a different viewfinder.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Feed shows black screen">
    - Ensure the viewfinder is in a loaded chunk
    - Check that the viewfinder hasn't been removed
    - Verify the TV is powered with redstone
    - Try relinking the cassette to the viewfinder
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tooltip says 'invalid location'">
    - The viewfinder may have been removed
    - The chunk may be in an unloaded dimension
    - Try relinking the cassette to a valid viewfinder
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't link to viewfinder">
    - Make sure you're right-clicking directly on the viewfinder block
    - Ensure you're holding the Hollow Cassette in your hand
    - Check that the viewfinder is properly placed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
