# Welcome to Vista

Vista is a Minecraft mod that adds functional television screens, security cameras, and cassette tapes to your world. Create surveillance systems, build home theaters, or record your favorite moments with this unique blend of retro technology and modern gameplay.

## How It Works

Vista introduces three main components that work together:

1. **Television Blocks** - Place them in the world and connect multiple blocks to form larger screens. Right-click with a cassette to play video content.

2. **Viewfinder Blocks** - Rotatable camera blocks that capture live feeds from their perspective. Adjust pitch and yaw to frame the perfect shot.

3. **Cassette System** - Two types of cassettes:
   - **Regular Cassettes** - Contain pre-recorded video animations
   - **Hollow Cassettes** - Link to viewfinder cameras for live broadcasts

## Requirements

- **Minecraft Version**: 1.21.1 or 1.21.2
- **Mod Loader**: Fabric or NeoForge
- **Dependencies**: 
  - Moonlight Lib (required)
  - Fabric API (Fabric only)
